Bio

I was born in a very beautiful city, Liuzhou, Guangxi province, China. I obtained my BSc at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in July 2017. Then, I became a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Mathematics of National University of Singapore (NUS) and earned my Ph.D. in December 2021, under the supervision of Professor Kim-Chuan Toh.

Current research interest

  • Large-scale computational optimization
  • Conic programming
  • Computational optimal transport
  • Nonlinear programming

Teaching

  • Graduate tutor for linear algebra (MA1101R), 2018-2020, National University of Singapore (Top tutor awards).
